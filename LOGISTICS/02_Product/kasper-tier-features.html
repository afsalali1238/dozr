Tier 2 Add-on
LVCAN200 CAN Adapter
Plugs into OBD port. Reads fuel, mileage, RPM, coolant from the vehicle's onboard computer. Light vehicles only.

ALLCAN300 CAN Adapter
Hardwired to CAN bus. 100+ parameters. For heavy construction equipment without an OBD port — excavators, cranes, dump trucks.
AED 387.41 landed · Hardwired installation
