🔑 On the Wialon reseller call — get these four answers

01
Wialon white-label price per device at 50 / 200 / 500 active units

This is the last unconfirmed number in the financial model. Get an exact AED figure. Under AED 5 → take Wialon. Over AED 8 → build custom platform.

02
Are you SIRA-certified? Can you be Kasper's installation partner?

If yes, this solves the Dubai compliance requirement in one conversation. Kasper doesn't need to self-certify in Phase 1.

03
Have you deployed ALLCAN300 on excavators or cranes in UAE?

Location Solution specialises in UAE construction fleet deployments. His experience tells you what installation complications to expect.

04
Can we run a 10-device pilot in the next 30 days?

The faster we get devices in the field, the faster the model starts generating revenue. A pilot also confirms the platform works before full commitment.

🖥️ For the tech team — three things to confirm

01
What does it cost to build the custom frontend on custom platform?

Read the Feature Specification (kasper-feature-spec.html) — 9 screens, 40+ features with dev notes per feature and DB schema. Quote based on that scope.

02
What AWS instance type does Teltonika recommend for 100 and 500 devices?

Email the Teltonika rep who shared the wiki links. Ask: "For custom platform receiving FMC130 data via Codec 8 Extended — what AWS instance and region do you recommend?"

03
custom platform or Wialon — what is your recommendation once Wialon price is known?

The decision rule: Wialon < AED 5/device → white-label for speed. Wialon > AED 8/device → build custom platform. Savings pay back build cost within 12 months at 500 devices.

After this call, the next step is clear: Get Wialon reseller pricing → decide platform → dev team writes SRS from the Feature Spec → install 10 pilot devices on existing vendors → start generating revenue. The business model is confirmed. Execution is what remains.
